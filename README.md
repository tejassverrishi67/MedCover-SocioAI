# MedCover-SocioAI

MedCover-SocioAI is a **student-built AI-powered web tool** designed to help users understand suitable health coverage options and government health schemes in India.

The project focuses on **clarity, accessibility, and explainability**, especially for users who find healthcare policies confusing or difficult to compare.

---

## ü©∫ Problem Statement

In India, many people struggle to understand:
- What kind of health insurance suits their personal and financial situation
- Whether they are eligible for government health schemes
- The trade-offs between private insurance and public healthcare support

This confusion is especially common among **low-income families**, **young earners**, and **people with existing medical conditions**.  
Making the wrong insurance decision can lead to serious financial stress during medical emergencies.

---

## üí° What We Built

MedCover-SocioAI is a simple, web-based tool that helps users make sense of healthcare options by:

- Collecting basic user details such as age, income, location, medical history, and dependents
- Analyzing this information in context instead of using rigid rules
- Suggesting **relevant health coverage options**, including:
  - Government health schemes
  - Private insurance plans
  - Hybrid approaches (e.g., employer-based coverage with add-ons)
- Clearly explaining **why each option is suggested** and what trade-offs the user should consider

The goal is **not to sell or recommend products**, but to help users **understand their options** and make informed decisions.

## ü§ñ How Artificial Intelligence Is Used

Artificial Intelligence is used to:
- Interpret financial, medical, and household details together (not in isolation)
- Compare the user‚Äôs situation with common real-world healthcare scenarios
- Generate **explainable reasoning**, such as:
  - Why a government scheme may be more suitable
  - Why private insurance could be risky or expensive in certain cases
  - What limitations or risks the user should be aware of
- Power a **context-aware chatbot** that answers follow-up questions using previously provided details

Instead of fixed IF‚ÄìELSE rules, the system uses **contextual reasoning** to adapt its analysis based on the user‚Äôs profile.
